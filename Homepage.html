General Information about Whitireia

Whitireia is committed to teaching what employers want you to know, in master's, postgraduate, degrees, diplomas, certificates and short course study. Our student support and learning resources are exceptional. At Whitireia, we offer multi-level qualifications from post school to postgraduate. Qualifications acquired at Whitireia are recognised and accepted in most countries of the world.

On this website you will find all of our short IT courses, taking you through a journey of quick tutorial on Microsoft Programs. These courses are very flexible in terms of time; they vary between 1 day - 5 days so they will not take up too much of your personal time! If you have any enquiry relating our company or the short courses, please don't hesitate to contact us!

Below are 2 of our most popular courses!

#1: Short Course on Microsoft Word for all ages!

Microsoft Word is one of the most commonly used computer programs in the world! Also named as Whitireia's most popular short course, it is guaranteed to leave you with extensive knowledge on the program.

Enrol yourself today!

#2: Short Course on Microsoft Powerpoint for Teenagers!

Ever find yourself struggling to control/create your powerpoint presentations? This course will introduce you to the fundamentals of the Microsoft Powerpoint program.
